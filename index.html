OUR AIM

We aim to empower women to feel as powerful as ever—strong enough to take on the parasites in our society, strong enough to overcome obstacles, and strong enough to defend themselves from any sexual attacks.

The system we plan to create will make it safer for women everywhere they go and at all times. A system that would restore humanity's high degree of sociability.

  • We are focused on building an effective, fast and trustworthy system to make Indian women feel safe and empowered.
  • Our platform will provide 24/7 active support and companionship so that women do not feel alone in crisis situations.
  • A real time women security system in public places which aims to provide a 100% safe environment

        WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE

VAIBILITY

Design with the user in mind: The platform will be made user-friendly and accessible to everyone by taking into account the demands and experiences of women. Women will find it simpler to use the app and more confidence in its ability to give them the support they need during times of crisis as a result.

EFFICIENCY

Active 24/7 support: The programme will give women in crisis circumstances with active 24/7 support, making sure they are never left alone and can always get help. Rapid and dependable response: The platform will be created to give women a rapid and dependable response in emergency situations, allowing them to get aid swiftly and effectively.
